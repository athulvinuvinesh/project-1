About

Celedon is a trusted home nursing company dedicated to delivering compassionate, professional, and personalized healthcare services in the comfort of your home. We believe that every individual deserves quality medical care without leaving the familiar surroundings of their home. Our experienced nurses provide skilled nursing care, medication management, wound dressing, post-surgical care, elderly care, palliative care, and chronic disease management with the highest level of professionalism. We focus on promoting faster recovery, improving overall well-being, and maintaining the dignity and independence of every patient. Each care plan is carefully tailored to meet the unique health needs of the individual and their family. Our caregivers are trained to offer not only medical assistance but also emotional support and companionship throughout the recovery journey. We are committed to ensuring patient safety, comfort, and continuous monitoring through reliable and compassionate care. Families can rely on our dedicated team to provide consistent healthcare services with empathy, respect, and integrity. By combining medical expertise with genuine compassion, Celedon creates a safe and supportive environment where patients receive exceptional care without the stress of hospital visits. Our mission is to make professional home nursing accessible, dependable, and comforting for every family we serve.

In addition to our specialized home nursing services, Celedon offers a complete range of home support solutions designed to simplify everyday living. Our trained housekeeping staff help maintain clean, organized, and hygienic homes that promote healthier lifestyles. We provide experienced electricians for safe electrical installations, repairs, maintenance, and emergency assistance whenever required. Our skilled plumbers handle plumbing installations, leak repairs, pipe maintenance, and water system services with efficiency and care. Reliable drivers are available for hospital visits, medical appointments, family transportation, and other travel needs, ensuring comfort and punctuality. We also offer professional caregivers, attendants, and domestic support staff to assist with daily household activities according to individual requirements. Every service is delivered by verified, trained, and trustworthy professionals who prioritize safety, quality, and customer satisfaction. Our goal is to become a single, dependable destination for both healthcare and essential home services, giving families complete peace of mind. Whether you need expert nursing care for a loved one or dependable assistance with maintaining your home, Celedon is committed to providing reliable solutions with professionalism and compassion. By bringing healthcare and home services together under one trusted name, Celedon makes everyday life safer, easier, and more comfortable for every family.
